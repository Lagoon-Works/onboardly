FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Some answers to some frequently asked questions.

How quickly can Onboardly deliver vetted candidates?

We typically deliver your first shortlist within 34 business days of the kickoff call. For urgent roles, we also run accelerated sourcing sprints to move even faster.

Can I request revisions or additional profiles?

Absolutely. You can request unlimited revisions, additional profiles, or replacement candidates until we find the perfect fit. We dont stop until youre genuinely satisfied.

What types of roles does Onboardly specialise in?

We specialize in Product, Sales, and Generalist roles for high-growth companies. Common roles include PM, APM, Product Owner, SDR/BDR, Account Executive, Customer Success, Chief of Staff, Founders Office & Operations.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Definitely. We work with seed-stage to Series C companies that need high-impact talent and tight turnaround times. Founding team & zero-to-one hiring is one of our strongest focus areas.

What if a candidate doesnt work out after joining?

We offer a 90-day risk-free replacement, no questions asked. Candidate success and retention matter more to us than just closure.
